Aurora Outdoors — Frequently Asked Questions (English)

Question: What are your shipping costs and delivery times?
Answer: Standard shipping within the EU costs 4.95 EUR and takes 2-4 business days. Orders over 75 EUR ship free. Express shipping (next business day) is available for 12.95 EUR on orders placed before 14:00 CET.

Question: Which countries do you ship to?
Answer: We ship to all EU countries, the UK, Switzerland, and Norway. Shipments outside the EU may incur customs duties, which are the customer's responsibility.

Question: What is your return policy?
Answer: You can return most items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, as long as they are unused and in their original packaging. Some hygiene-related items such as water bottles and mouthpieces are excluded from return. To start a return, ask our assistant to "return an item" and we will guide you through it.

Question: How long does a refund take after I return an item?
Answer: Once your return arrives at our warehouse, inspection takes 1-2 business days. The refund is then issued to your original payment method and typically appears within 3-5 business days, depending on your bank.

Question: How can I track my order?
Answer: Once your order ships, you receive a tracking number by email. You can also ask our assistant to check your order status at any time — just have your order number and email address ready.

Question: Can I change or cancel my order after placing it?
Answer: Orders can be changed or cancelled free of charge as long as they are still in "Processing" status. Once an order has shipped, it can no longer be cancelled, but you can return it after delivery.

Question: What payment methods do you accept?
Answer: We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Klarna (pay later and installments), and SEPA direct debit for registered customers.

Question: Do your products come with a warranty?
Answer: All Aurora Outdoors products come with the statutory 2-year warranty. Our own Aurora-brand tents, backpacks, and jackets additionally carry a 5-year manufacturer warranty covering materials and workmanship.

Question: How do I choose the right size for jackets and boots?
Answer: Every product page includes a detailed size chart. Our jackets generally run true to size; if you are between sizes, we recommend sizing up for layering. For hiking boots, we recommend ordering half a size larger than your street shoes to allow for hiking socks.

Question: How should I care for my waterproof jacket?
Answer: Wash your waterproof jacket at 30°C with a technical fabric detergent, without fabric softener. Reactivate the water-repellent (DWR) coating by tumble drying on low heat for 20 minutes or ironing on low with a cloth between iron and jacket.

Question: Do you have physical stores?
Answer: Aurora Outdoors is online-only, which lets us keep prices low. We do host seasonal pop-up stores in Berlin, Munich, and Amsterdam — dates are announced in our newsletter.

Question: How can I reach human customer support?
Answer: Our support team is available Monday to Friday, 09:00-18:00 CET via this chat (ask for a support ticket or a human agent) or by email at support@aurora-outdoors.example. Our assistant can create a support ticket for you at any time, 24/7.
